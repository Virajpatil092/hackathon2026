## Key Insights

### 💰 Highest Impact Spending Categories
1. **Fuel (5541)** - 2.31 kgCO2e per EUR
   - Single most impactful category
   - 9× higher than dining
   
2. **Groceries (5411, 5499)** - 0.70 kgCO2e per EUR
   - Food production and transportation
   - Higher for meat-heavy purchases

3. **Dining (5812-5816)** - 0.60 kgCO2e per EUR
   - Restaurant meals have higher footprint than home cooking
   - Varies by restaurant type (fast food vs fine dining)

### ✅ Lowest Impact Categories
1. **Telecom & Financial (4814, 4829, 4899)** - 0.15 kgCO2e per EUR
2. **Healthcare & Pharmacy (591x, 80xx, 806x)** - 0.20 kgCO2e per EUR
3. **Transport & Travel (411x, 421x, 451x)** - 0.25 kgCO2e per EUR

### 🎯 Recommendations for Reduction
- **Reduce fuel spending** - Switch to EV, public transport, or car-sharing
- **Choose groceries over dining** - Cook at home instead of eating out
- **Public transport** - Use buses, trains (more efficient than personal vehicles)
- **Online services** - Digital products have lower footprint than physical goods
